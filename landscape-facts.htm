Facts about the geography of the Earth

  1. Continents shift at about the same rate as your fingernails grow.
  2. Ninety percent of Earth's population lives in the Northern Hemisphere.
  3. In the Philippines, there’s an island that’s within a lake, on an island that’s within a lake, on an island.
  4. Vatican City is the smallest country in the world.
  5. Africa is the only continent that covers four hemispheres.
  6. Mount Everest, the world’s tallest mountain, can fit inside the Marianas Trench, the deepest part of the ocean.
  7. The Dead Sea is currently 429 meters below sea level and sinking about 1 meter a year.
  8. Australia is wider than the moon.
